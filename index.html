✦ About the Ministry

Abigael Glory Ministries was founded by Besong Aseck Lydia — a Spirit-filled Digital Christian Evangelist called to carry the light of Christ across the internet and into the hardest places of society.

Born out of personal hardship, lack, and an unquenchable burden for souls, Lydia began preaching in her community and launched her Facebook ministry in 2025, quickly reaching over 5,000 followers. Through Gospel Blast, teachings on the Life of Jesus, Journey to Easter, and upcoming projects Abigael and Sophia, the ministry is touching lives across Cameroon and beyond.

🌟 Real Testimonies

🏥 Physical Healing

In 2024, during our ministry posting in Nyom, Cameroon, a beloved mama was diagnosed with breast cancer. We gathered and prayed in the name of Jesus. God moved. She was miraculously healed — to this day she stands as a living testimony. All glory to Jesus.

🙏

✨ Healing Miracle

In 2023 in Nkolodom, Yaoundé, a taxi driver had been bedridden for two years with a severe foot ulcer. Two weeks after prayer, the ulcer completely dried up and healed. The family offered their home as a church and gave their lives to Christ. A healing became a church.

✨
Anonymous — Taxi DriverNkolodom, Yaoundé · 2023
